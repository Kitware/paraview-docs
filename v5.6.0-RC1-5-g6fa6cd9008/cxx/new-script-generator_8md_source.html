1 # New Catalyst Script Definition and Export
2 
3 There is a new streamlined process for defining and exporting
4 Catalyst scripts. The Catalyst Export Inspector panel is the new
5 place to define everything you want Catalyst to eventually export.
6 Your choices are saveable in ParaView State files and match the
7 normal UI for exporting data files and screenshots. There are some
8 new options too, including choices to export Cinema Spec D tables
9 and to place all Catalyst output inside a specific directory. To
10 export the Catalyst Script you've defined, use the new Export
11 Catalyst Script option.
12 
13 Both entry points are under the Catalyst menu. The original
14 Export State wizard and Data Extract Writers controls are still
15 there too but they are deprecated and will be removed soon.
