New Palette Backgrounds

Palette backgrounds have been reorganized and a few new palettes are introduced.

  • "Gray Background" is renamed "Blue Gray Background (Default)"
  • "Default Background" and "Print Background" are removed (they are duplicates of "Blue Gray Background" and "White Background"respectively)
  • "Warm Gray Background", "Neutral Gray Background" and "Light Gray Background" are new available palette backgrounds.
