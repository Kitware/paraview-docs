Configurable max radius polar axis representation

You can now configure a custom max radius for polar axis representation. Note that by default the custom max radius is off and therefore automatically computed relatively to pole position.

The protected member variable vtkPolarAxesRepresentation::EnableCustomRadius and its related methods SetEnableCustomRadius and GetEnableCustomRadius have been deprecated. They are replaced by SetEnableCustomMinRadius and GetEnableCustomMinRadius methods to maintain consistency in naming.
