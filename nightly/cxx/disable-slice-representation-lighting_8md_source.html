1 # Slice representation lighting is turned off
2 
3 Lighting on the Slice representation has the undesirable effect of
4 darkening the Slice with the default light, which is off center with
5 respect to the view and focal points. We assume the common use case
6 for this representation is to view the data as directly as possible to
7 match colors on the slice with colors in the color legend. To better
8 support this use case, lighting is disabled for this representation.
