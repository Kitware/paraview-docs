1 ## Coordinate Frame Widget
2 
3 ParaView now has a widget for manipulating coordinate frames.
4 A coordinate frame is all the information needed to uniquely specify
5 a linear transformation:
6 
7 + a base point in world coordinates
8 + three ortho-normal vectors defining new coordinate axes in terms of the existing world coordinates.
9 
10 If you have a group of properties containing 4 double-vectors, you can
11 add `panel_widget="InteractiveFrame"` to the group's XML attributes and
12 it will appear in the properties panel as a `pqCoordinateFramePropertyWidget`.
13 
14 See [this VTK discourse topic](https://discourse.vtk.org/t/vtkcoordinateframewidget/) for
15 more information about how to use the widget.
