1 ## New option in the scalar bar to show displayed range values
2 
3 The range values of the visible scalar fields can now be displayed on the scalar bar.
4 To do so, click on `Edit Color Map` icon, then in the new menu, click on
5 `Edit color legend properties` icon. If the checkbox `Show Range` is on, then the range will be
6 printed next to the color bar.
7 
8 The range printed is composed of a Min value, and a Max value. The Min value (resp. Max value)
9 is the minimum (resp. maxmimum) value of
10 all combined arrays that share the same name and are visible in the view
11 (with an open eyeball in the pipeline browser).
