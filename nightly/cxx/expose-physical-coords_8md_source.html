1 ## Expose physical cooridnate system
2 
3 This change exposes on render view proxy the `SetPhysicalToWorldMatrix` method
4 (via a new `PhysicalToWorldMatrix` property), which is recently available on the
5 `vtkRenderWindow` class in VTK.
6 
7 Some common representations have been updated to be aware of the already-existing
8 coordinate system api on underlying actors, via a new representation proxy property
9 (called `CoordinateSystem`). This property allows users to place objects in World
10 or Physical coords.
11 
12 Also `vtkPVLODActor` and `vtkPVLODVolume` have been updated to be aware that they
13 can be in non-world coordinate systems, and to recompute bounds when that is the
14 case.
