7 # Notes
8 
9 ## Support for 12-node wedges in IOSS
10 
11 The IOSS reader now supports mixed-order, 12-node wedge elements.
12 These elements have quadratic triangle faces but quadrilaterals
13 with 2 linear sides and are sometimes used to represent material
14 failure along a shared boundary between two tetrahedra (i.e.,
15 the wedge is assigned a zero or negligible stiffness, allowing
16 the neighboring tetrahedra to move relative to one another without
17 inducing strain.
18 
19 Bump VTK to support vtkQuadraticLinearWedge in IOSS. [(details)](https://gitlab.kitware.com/paraview/paraview/-/merge_requests/6215).
20 
21 ## Point Interpolator Null Points Strategy Typo Fix
22 
23 The PointInterpolatorBase source proxy had a typo that prevented users from
24 selecting "Closest Point" as the Null Points strategy for the Point Line Interpolator,
25 Point Plane Interpolator, Point Volume Interpolator, and Point Dataset Interpolator
26 Filters. Note that this only affects those interpolation kernels that can produce
27 such null points, such as the Linear Kernel while using a radius based interpolation.
28 
29 Bug: PointInterpolatorBase NullPointsStrategy Typo [(details)](https://gitlab.kitware.com/paraview/paraview/-/merge_requests/6177)
30 
31 ## pqLoadDataReaction bug with default file readers
32 
33 pqLoadDataReaction was fixed to trigger the loadData() signal when a default reader was used as specified in Paraview-UserSettings.json.
