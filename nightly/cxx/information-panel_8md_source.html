1 ## Information Panel
2 
3 The **Information Panel** has been redesigned to improve the way information is
4 presented to the users. Besides general layout improvements that make the panel
5 more compact and consistent with other panels, the changes are as follows:
6 
7 * When showing composite datasets, one can view information specific to a subtree by
8  selecting that subtree in the panel. However, if the data changes, for
9  example, due to animation, the selection was lost making it tedious to monitor
10  data information changes for a specific subtree. This has been fixed.
11 
12 * All numbers now use locale specific formatting. For example, for US-EN based
13  locale, large number are formatting by placing commas. Memory used is shown in
14  KB, MB, or GB as appropriate.
