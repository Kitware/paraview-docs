1 # MooseXfemClip Plugin
2 
3 The MooseXfemClip filter has been added as a plugin to aid
4 in the visualization of results produced using the XFEM module
5 in the MOOSE framework (http://www.mooseframework.org)
6 developed at Idaho National Laboratory.
7 
8 The MOOSE XFEM implementation uses a phantom node approach
9 to represent discontinuities in finite element solutions,
10 which results in overlapping finite elements. The MooseXfemClip
11 filter clips off the non-physical portions of the overlapping
12 elements, allowing correct visualization of the discontinuous
13 fields produced by this method.
